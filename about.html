My Life

Jelisaveta Stojanovic

Jelisaveta Stojanović, professionally known as DJ Saveta, is a talented and versatile electronic music artist who has made a significant mark on the music scene. With a passion for mixing and a keen sense of rhythm, Jelisaveta has captivated audiences with her dynamic and energetic performances.

Born and raised in a musically rich environment, Jelisaveta's love for music developed at a young age. Drawing inspiration from various genres, she embarked on her journey as a DJ, exploring the world of electronic dance music. Her innate ability to connect with the crowd and curate seamless sets has earned her recognition as a sought-after DJ in both local and international events.

Jelisaveta's signature sound is a fusion of deep and progressive beats, skillfully blended with melodic and atmospheric elements. Her sets often take listeners on a transformative journey, creating an immersive experience on the dance floor. Her dedication to refining her craft and pushing musical boundaries has gained her a loyal fanbase and the respect of fellow artists within the electronic music community.

Beyond her performances, DJ Jelisaveta is also a fervent advocate for the advancement of electronic music culture. She actively engages in collaborative projects, showcases emerging talent, and contributes to the growth of the electronic music scene through her unique artistic vision.

With her remarkable talent, boundless creativity, and an unwavering commitment to the art of DJing, Jelisaveta Stojanović continues to evolve as a prominent figure in the world of electronic music, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of music enthusiasts worldwide.
